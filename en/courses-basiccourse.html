Basic Course

Course Description

This course is designed for beginners and covers the fundamentals of artificial intelligence. Over 5 weeks, you will learn the basic concepts of machine learning, get familiar with Python, and explore key aspects of AI. The course includes both theoretical knowledge and practical work, allowing you to immediately apply what you learn. Even if you have no prior experience in this field, the structured and easy-to-understand approach will help you grasp the material.

What Will You Learn in This Course?

In this course, you will learn the basics of Python, key concepts of machine learning, and acquire skills that will allow you to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world projects. You will learn how to develop simple models, analyze data, and utilize machine learning techniques for data processing.

Additionally, the course helps you develop analytical thinking and problem-solving skills using ML. Practical tasks will enable you to gain a deeper understanding of machine learning principles and how they can be applied across different industries.

Why Is This Course Useful?

  • Low entry level — the course is suitable for beginners with no prior experience.

  • Practical focus — you will apply your knowledge right away.

  • Step-by-step learning of complex concepts with clear explanations.

By the end of the course, you will be prepared to continue studying advanced AI topics or transition to related fields such as machine learning or data analytics.

Course Curriculum

This course introduces you to the fundamentals of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Over 5 weeks, you will learn the basic concepts of AI, work with Python, and develop simple models. The course integrates theory and practice, allowing you to immediately apply your knowledge. It is designed for beginners with no prior experience, providing clear and step-by-step instruction.
