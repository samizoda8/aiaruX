TechWomen and AI Academy: Encouraging Women in STEM

In April 2024, AI Academy, in collaboration with TechWomen, held an important event aimed at supporting women in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) in Tajikistan. This event was part of the official TechWomen delegation in Dushanbe and brought together participants from various sectors, ranging from young professionals to experienced mentors.

TechWomen is an initiative of the U.S. Department of State that supports women in technology by providing access to mentoring, professional development, and international collaborations. As part of its mission, TechWomen helps participants from Central Asia and other regions find new professional development paths and expand their influence in STEM.

Main highlights of the event
Technology Education and Career Prospects
During the AI Academy event, participants had the unique opportunity to engage with TechWomen representatives and leading experts from various fields. The program included the following segments:
Interactive Lectures – speakers discussed the development of technologies, artificial intelligence, and future digital careers.
Roundtable – key trends in AI and fintech, including innovative solutions in machine learning, were discussed.
Workshops – participants learned about building a successful career in STEM and the use of AI in developing new products.

The AI Academy and TechWomen event in Dushanbe marked another significant step in the development of technological education in Tajikistan. The stories of inspiring participants and the practical advice from leading experts help young women confidently move forward in the world of technology.
